About Kirsten

Kirsten is a 26 year old professional dancer born in New Zealand, but raised in Cape Town South Africa. A Hip Hop International world champion at the age of 13, Kirsten has built her repertoire to include big names like Jennier Lopez, Justin Beiber, and Rihanna. She is famous for her performance in Justin Beiber's iconic music video 'Sorry', but she is most know for starring in performances for the Royal Family Dance Crew, recognized as the best international dance crew in the world. Most recently she was seen on a Korean dance reality called Street Woman Fighters (SWF), representing Jam Republic Agency as the leader of the dance crew Jam Republic.

Check out Jam Republic's Website to learn about the efforts being made to share dance with the world!
